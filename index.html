QueueR
QueueR is an Android app to be used by restaurants and their customers. Restaurants sign up for an account, which generates for them a unique QR code that they can display in front of their restaurants. Customers can then use the app to scan this QR code to enter themselves onto the restaurant's waitlist. The customer's client then displays their position in line and they will receive a push notification for when their table is ready. On the restaurant's client, they are able to manage the wait list by setting statuses (waiting, seated, finished) to the entries.

To handle the account authentication, the database, and the push notifications, Google Firebase was used. To generate, scan, and decode the QR codes, a library called ZXing was used.

ConductR
Ever in the middle of doing something productive online when a bad song comes up and ruins your vibe? Tired of having to switch tabs to play the next video on YouTube? Well, ConductR is the solution for you! With ConductR, you are the conductor for your own orchestra. This web app uses your webcam along with your hand gestures to manipulate a YouTube playlist that you enter. Wave right to play the next video, wave left to play the previous, wave up to stop, and wave down to shuffle. It's that easy!

Developed using Bootstrap for the frontend, the YouTube API, and a library called Gest.js to recognize hand movements.
